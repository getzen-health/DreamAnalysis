AntarAI — Tab Structure Options

Pick the structure that feels right. Each one is a different way to organize the same features.

A

Bevel-Style: Score First

Tabs: Today | Discover | [Mic] | Nutrition | You


Today — Single readiness score (0-100), mood ring, stress gauge, sleep quality, quick stats cards

Discover — Brain monitor, inner energy, neurofeedback, meditation, dreams, AI companion (card grid)

Nutrition — Food log, macro rings, calorie tracking, craving analysis, meal history

You — Profile photo + name, streak badge, session history, health connections, devices, preferences, export data, privacy, help, logout

B

Apple Health-Style: Categories

Tabs: Summary | Mind | [Mic] | Body | You


Summary — Daily highlights: top emotion, stress level, sleep score, nutrition score, favorites pinned

Mind — Emotion state, trends chart, inner energy, meditation depth, AI companion, dreams

Body — Sleep analysis, food log + cravings, health metrics (HR/HRV/steps), neurofeedback

You — Profile + avatar, weekly streaks, personal records, session archive, connected devices, health sync, settings, privacy policy, about AntarAI

C

Minimal: 3 Tabs + Mic

Tabs: Home | [Mic] | You


Home — Everything in one scrollable feed: readiness score, emotion card, sleep card, nutrition card, AI insight, discover section (horizontal scroll cards to sub-pages)

You — Full profile hub: avatar + bio, streaks + achievements, emotion history, session archive, connected devices (Google Health / Muse), food log history, settings + preferences, export, privacy, help

All detail pages (brain monitor, inner energy, dreams, etc.) are sub-pages from Home cards
