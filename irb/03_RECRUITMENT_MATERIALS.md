## DOCUMENT D: SCREENING SURVEY QUESTIONS

> **Usage:** Create this as a REDCap survey, Google Form, or Typeform. Keep it brief (under 2 minutes). Only collect what is needed to determine eligibility.

---

**Title:** EEG Research Study — Quick Eligibility Check (2 minutes)

**Introduction text:**
Thank you for your interest in our research study! This quick survey helps us determine if you qualify to participate. Your answers will only be used to assess eligibility. All responses are confidential. If you qualify, we will contact you within 3 business days to schedule your first session.

---

**Questions:**

1. How old are you?
   - Under 18 *(→ Ineligible)*
   - 18–65 ✓
   - Over 65 *(→ Ineligible)*

2. Do you speak English fluently enough to have a conversation and understand written instructions in English?
   - Yes ✓
   - No *(→ Ineligible)*

3. Have you ever been diagnosed with epilepsy or a seizure disorder, or have you ever had a seizure?
   - No ✓
   - Yes *(→ Ineligible)*

4. Have you ever been diagnosed with any of the following? (Select all that apply)
   - Parkinson's disease *(→ Ineligible)*
   - Traumatic brain injury (TBI) *(→ Flag for review)*
   - Dementia or Alzheimer's disease *(→ Ineligible)*
   - Schizophrenia or another psychotic disorder *(→ Ineligible)*
   - None of the above ✓

5. Are you currently taking any of the following medications? (Select all that apply)
   - Anticonvulsants (e.g., Keppra, Lamictal, Depakote) *(→ Ineligible)*
   - Antipsychotics (e.g., Seroquel, Risperdal, Abilify) *(→ Ineligible)*
   - Benzodiazepines (e.g., Xanax, Valium, Klonopin) *(→ Ineligible)*
   - None of the above ✓
   - Other (I'm not sure) *(→ Flag for follow-up)*

6. Do you have any scalp conditions, open wounds, or skin problems on your forehead or the area above your ears that would make it uncomfortable to wear a headset?
   - No ✓
   - Yes *(→ Flag for review)*

7. Are you currently pregnant?
   - No ✓
   - Yes *(→ Ineligible)*
   - Prefer not to say *(→ Follow up verbally)*

8. Have you participated in another EEG research study in the past 30 days?
   - No ✓
   - Yes *(→ Flag for review — may still be eligible depending on other study)*

9. Are you able to sit still and wear a headband on your forehead for approximately 45 minutes?
   - Yes ✓
   - No *(→ Ineligible)*

---

**Contact information (collected after eligibility confirmed):**

10. What is your first name? *(for scheduling purposes only)*
11. What is your email address?
12. What is your phone number? (optional)
13. Do you prefer remote (video call) or in-person sessions?
    - Remote only
    - In-person only
    - Either works

14. What time zones/general availability works for you? (free text)

---

**Thank you message (eligible):**
"Thank you! Based on your answers, you appear to qualify for this study. We will contact you at the email address you provided within 3 business days to schedule your first session."

**Thank you message (ineligible):**
"Thank you for your interest! Unfortunately, based on your answers, you do not meet the eligibility criteria for this study at this time. This does not reflect on you in any way — eligibility criteria exist only to ensure the scientific validity of our findings."
