| 2026-03-20 | neuroscience-papers | Neurofeedback system has **no cross-session progress tracking or dosing guidance**. Clinical neurofeedback requires 20-40 sessions for neuroplastic effects (Gruzelier 2014, Enriquez-Geppert 2019: 25-30 sessions for ADHD), optimal dosing 2-3x/week with >=1 day spacing, and gaps >2 weeks cause skill decay (Hammond 2011).
